Information about application:

Registration

Register your account in our system to share your music with others!
Please provide login, email and password twice in our form.

Login

Login if you want to add and rate some music!

 My account

You can check your account details here.

 Songs

List all songs in our system. You can filter them by band name and genre.
Page pagination works here too!

Add song

Use this panel to add your song to the system.
You need to provide song's title, band name and lyrics.
You can also add clip from youtube and set genre.

Other users

You can see users which are registered in our system!
Just click on the cell to go to user's details. Here you can see all user's songs!
